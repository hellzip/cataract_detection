How To Guide

  1. Open the Home page and click "Add Photo".
  2. Upload a clear photo of your eye, ensuring that the pupil is visible.
  3. Adjust the detection circle to align with your pupil, then click "Detect".
  4. Wait for the system to analyze the photo and display the result.
