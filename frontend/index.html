How to Get Started with Cataract Detection

  1. Capture an Eye Photo: Point your device's camera at your eye, ensuring the pupil area is clearly visible. Follow the on-screen guide for optimal results.
  2. Adjust the Photo: Align and focus the detection circle on the pupil area.
  3. Click "Detect": The app will process the image and provide the detection results.
  4. View Detection Results: The results will include preliminary information about your eye condition.
